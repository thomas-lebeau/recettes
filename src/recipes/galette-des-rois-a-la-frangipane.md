# Galette des rois à la frangipane

**Prep:** 10 min / **Cook:** 20 min / **Total:** 30 min

## Ingredients

- 100 g fine sugar
- 75 g soft butter
- 2 sheets puff pastry
- 140 g ground almonds
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 fève (charm/prize token)

## Directions

1. Place one puff pastry sheet in a tart mold and prick it with a fork.
2. In a bowl, mix the ground almonds, sugar, 2 eggs, and soft butter.
3. Spread the almond mixture in the tart mold and hide the fève inside.
4. Cover with the second puff pastry sheet, sealing the edges firmly.
5. Make decorative patterns on the surface and brush with egg yolk.
6. Bake for 20-30 minutes at 200°C (thermometer 6-7); check regularly for doneness.
