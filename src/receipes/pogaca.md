# Poğaça

## Ingredients

- 1 packet yeast
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sunflower oil
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 500g flour
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Mix yeast and sugar.
2. Mix warm water with milk and add to the mixture.
3. Add oil, salt and flour.
4. Brush with egg yolk.
5. Bake until golden.
