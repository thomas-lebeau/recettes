# Tarte au citron

**Prep:** 10 min / **Cook:** 30 min

## Ingredients

- 1 shortcrust pastry
- 200g sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 75g butter
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Melt the butter.
3. Squeeze the lemons and grate the zest.
4. Beat 150g of sugar with the 2 whole eggs until the mixture whitens. Add the butter, zest and lemon juice.
5. Pour onto the pie crust and bake for 30 minutes.
6. When the tart is baked, beat the egg whites until stiff and gradually add the remaining sugar (50g) to obtain a firm meringue.
7. Spread the meringue on the baked tart and return to the oven to brown for a few minutes.
