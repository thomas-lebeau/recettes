# Mercimek çorbası

## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon tomato or pepper paste
- 1 carrot in small cubes
- 2 cups red lentils
- 2 liters hot water
- Salt, pepper, and cumin

## Directions

1. Cook for 30 minutes.
2. Mix with blender until smooth.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and cumin.
