# Gnocchis au feta et legumes roti

## Ingredients

- 500g gnocchi
- 1 zucchini, cut into half-rounds
- 1 red onion, roughly chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 4-5 sprigs fresh thyme
- 250g feta cheese
- Cooking oil
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 450Â°F.
2. In a large bowl, add the gnocchi, zucchini pieces, red onion, garlic, tomatoes, maple syrup, thyme, then pour a good drizzle of oil, a pinch of salt and pepper, and mix gently.
3. Transfer everything to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, add the feta, another drizzle of oil and a pinch of pepper.
4. Bake for 25 minutes.
5. Remove from the oven, then using a fork, crush the feta cheese, then mix everything gently. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve.
