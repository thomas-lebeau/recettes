# Espinacas y garbanzos al curry con leche de coco

**Total:** 25 min

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 250 g fresh spinach
- 350 g crushed tomatoes
- 350 g canned chickpeas (drained)
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2-3 teaspoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- Olive oil
- Salt and freshly ground pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and chop the onion and garlic cloves. Wash the spinach leaves.

2. Heat the oil in a pan. Add the spices and stir for 1 minute to release all the aromas. Add the onion and garlic and saut√© for 2-3 minutes. Add a little water if necessary.

3. Add the crushed tomatoes, coconut milk, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil. Add the chickpeas, lower the heat, and simmer for about 10 minutes.

4. Next, add the spinach leaves and cook for 5 more minutes.
