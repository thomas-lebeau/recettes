# Curry de nouilles aux crevettes

**Prep:** 15 min / **Cook:** 10 min / **Total:** 25 min

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 500g peeled shrimp
- 500g Chinese noodles
- 400ml coconut milk
- 3 teaspoons curry powder
- Soy sauce
- Oil
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the noodles in a pot of boiling water following the package instructions.
2. Meanwhile, heat 3 tablespoons of oil in a wok. Saut√© the shrimp until golden, then add soy sauce to taste and mix well. Add the coconut milk, curry, a little pepper and mix again.
3. When the noodles are cooked, drain them well and add them to the wok. Let cook for a few more minutes then serve immediately.
