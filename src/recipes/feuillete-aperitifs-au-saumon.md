# Feuilletés apéritifs au saumon

**Prep:** 15 min / **Freeze:** 60 min / **Cook:** 20 min / **Total:** 95 min

**Serves:** 20 pieces

## Ingredients

- 1 puff pastry sheet
- 4 large slices of smoked salmon
- 1 small bunch of fresh chives
- 100 ml (⅓ cup + 1½ tbsp) thick cream (crème fraîche)
- Fleur de sel
- Freshly ground pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the chives finely.
2. Roll out the puff pastry into a rectangle (or trim the edges if using a pre-rolled sheet).
3. Spread the cream evenly over the pastry.
4. Sprinkle with the chopped chives.
5. Cover with the smoked salmon slices.
6. Sprinkle lightly with fleur de sel and pepper.
7. Roll the pastry into a log, wrap tightly in plastic wrap, and freeze for 1 hour.
8. Cut the log into slices about 0.5 cm (¼ inch) thick.
9. Arrange the slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
10. Bake for about 20 minutes at 180°C (350°F) until lightly golden.
