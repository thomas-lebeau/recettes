# Quiche provençale

**Prep:** 30 min / **Cook:** 45 min / **Total:** 1h15

**Serves:** 6

## Ingredients

- 1 puff pastry
- 1 small pot of crème fraîche
- 2 onions
- 1 bell pepper
- 3 tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper
- 3 eggs
- 100 g gruyère cheese
- 1 can of tuna
- 10 pitted green olives
- Provençal herbs

## Directions

1. Unroll the puff pastry, keeping the parchment paper, and spread it in a tart pan.
2. Cut the 3 tomatoes into rounds. Arrange them nicely in the pan. Refrigerate.
3. In a bowl, cut the onion into small dice and the bell pepper into thin strips.
4. Add the tuna flakes, 3 eggs, crème fraîche, gruyère cheese, and olives cut into strips.
5. Season with salt and pepper. Add a bit of Provençal herbs. Mix well.
6. Spread the mixture over the tart. Bake for 45 minutes at 180°C (350°F).
7. Serve slightly warm accompanied by a salad.
