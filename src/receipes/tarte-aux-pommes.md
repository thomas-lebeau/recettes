# Tarte aux pommes

## Ingredients

- 2-3 apples
- 100-150g sugar
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 eggs
- 100g liquid cream (optional)
- Ground cinnamon
- A little milk

## Directions

1. Spread the peeled apple slices on a pie crust.
2. Prepare a cream with the sugar, flour, eggs and milk and pour over the apples.
3. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
4. Bake for 20-30 minutes.
