# Riz Mexicain

## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- Bacon lardons
- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 yellow bell pepper
- Tomatoes
- Rice
- 1 chorizo

## Directions

1. Sauté an onion in olive oil, then remove.
2. Sauté the bacon lardons, then remove.
3. Add the onions, lardons, salt and pepper and simmer on low heat.
4. Add water (2 volumes for 1 volume of rice). When boiling, add the rice and chorizo cut into rounds.
5. Cook for about 20 minutes.
